\revadd{Notes on TBM 2 to teams/referee/organizer:\\}
\revadd{The cover plates are organized in the conveyor belt with the order of \textbf{unusable-unusable-faulty-faulty} (yes, specifically in this order).
The reasoning is because processing unusable cover plate is ``simpler" than processing faulty ones.
As such the only way to ensure fairness is to have the same ordering of the cover plates.}
